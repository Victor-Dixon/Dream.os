- **Realistic**: $180,000 (10 events/month avg)
- **Optimistic**: $252,000 (14 events/month avg)

### Cost Structure

#### Fixed Costs (Monthly):
- Insurance: $150
- Marketing: $400
- Software/Tools: $100
- Website/Email: $50
- Vehicle/Equipment Lease: $300
- **Total Fixed**: ~$1,000/month

#### Variable Costs (Per Event):
- Bartender wages: $200-400 (20-40% of event fee)
- Alcohol/Mixers: 15-25% of event fee
- Supplies/Garnishes: 5-10% of event fee
- Travel: $25-75
- **Total Variable**: ~40-55% of revenue per event

### Profitability Analysis

**Break-Even Point**: 
- Fixed costs: $1,000/month
- Variable costs: ~45% of revenue
- Break-even revenue: ~$1,820/month (1-2 events)
- **Target break-even**: Month 6-8

**Profit Margins**:
- Gross Margin: 55-60% (after variable costs)
- Net Margin: 35-45% (after all costs)
- Target net profit: $63,000-81,000/year (Year 1)
