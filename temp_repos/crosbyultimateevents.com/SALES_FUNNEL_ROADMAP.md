# Sales Funnel Roadmap: Crosby Ultimate Events
## Based on Business Plan Analysis

**Document Date:** December 2024  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## Executive Summary

This sales funnel roadmap outlines a strategic customer journey designed to convert prospects into clients and retain them for repeat business. The funnel is structured around the five-stage customer lifecycle: Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Action → Retention.

---

## Sales Funnel Overview

### Funnel Stages

```
Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Action → Retention
   ↓           ↓            ↓            ↓          ↓
Website    Consultation  Proposal    Booking   Follow-up
Social      Content      Packages   Contract   Reviews
SEO         Email        Pricing    Payment    Referrals
Ads         Lead Mag    Testimonial  Service   Repeat
```

---

## Stage 1: AWARENESS (Top of Funnel - TOFU)

### Objective
Attract potential clients and build brand awareness

### Target Audience
- Affluent professionals & families ($150K+ income, 35-65 age)
- Corporate clients (team building, client entertainment)
- Special occasion planners (weddings, anniversaries, birthdays)
- Health-conscious consumers (dietary restrictions)

### Marketing Channels & Tactics

#### 1. Website (crosbyultimateevents.com)
**Current Status:** Basic WordPress theme exists  
**Improvements Needed:**
- Hero section with compelling value proposition
- Service showcase sections
- Portfolio/gallery of events
- SEO optimization (local search keywords)
- Clear call-to-action buttons (CTA)
- Lead capture forms

**Key Pages:**
- Homepage (hero + services + CTA)
- Services page (detailed service descriptions)
- Portfolio/Gallery (visual proof)
- About page (brand story, credentials)
- Contact/Consultation booking page
- Blog (content marketing)

#### 2. SEO Strategy
**Keywords to Target:**
- "private chef [city/region]"
- "event planning services [city/region]"
- "in-home dining experiences"
- "corporate catering [city/region]"
- "wedding event planner [city/region]"

**Local SEO:**
- Google My Business listing
- Local directory listings (Yelp, Yellow Pages)
- Location-specific landing pages
- Client location testimonials

#### 3. Social Media Presence
**Platforms:**
- **Instagram**: Visual content (food photography, event setups, behind-the-scenes)
  - Posting frequency: 3-5 times per week
  - Content types: Food photos, event setups, client testimonials, recipes
  - Hashtags: #privatechef #eventplanning #culinaryexperience

- **Facebook**: Community building, event promotion
  - Business page with reviews
  - Event listings and promotions
  - Community engagement

- **Pinterest**: Recipe and event inspiration
  - Visual boards for different event types
  - Recipe pins linking to blog

- **LinkedIn**: B2B corporate client outreach
  - Professional content
  - Corporate event case studies
  - Industry networking

#### 4. Online Advertising
**Google Ads:**
- Local search campaigns
- Service-specific keywords
- Budget: $500-$1,000/month initially

**Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
- Targeted demographics (income, age, interests)
- Lookalike audiences from website visitors
- Retargeting campaigns
- Budget: $300-$800/month initially

**Yelp/Directory Ads:**
- Local business listings
- Premium placement
- Budget: $200-$500/month

### Conversion Goals (Stage 1)
- Website visitors: 500-1,000/month (Year 1)
- Social media followers: 1,000+ (combined platforms)
- Lead generation: 30-50 inquiries/month

### Key Metrics
- Website traffic (sessions, page views)
- Social media engagement (likes, shares, comments)
- Cost per click (CPC) on ads
- Brand awareness metrics

## Stage 2: INTEREST (Middle of Funnel - MOFU)

### Objective
Nurture prospects and build interest in services

### Lead Capture & Nurturing

#### 1. Lead Magnets
**Free Resources Offered:**
- "Ultimate Event Planning Checklist" (PDF download)
- "10 Signature Recipes from Our Private Chef" (email series)
- "Corporate Event Planning Guide" (B2B focused)
- "Dietary Restriction Menu Ideas" (PDF)

**Capture Method:**
- Email signup forms on website
- Landing pages with lead magnets
- Social media lead ads

#### 2. Email Marketing Campaigns
**Welcome Series (5 emails over 2 weeks):**
1. Welcome + brand introduction
2. Service overview + portfolio highlights
3. Client success stories/testimonials
4. Special offer/promotion
5. Consultation invitation

**Nurturing Campaigns:**
- Monthly newsletter with event tips, recipes
- Seasonal promotions (holidays, wedding season)
- Educational content (event planning tips)
- Client spotlights and case studies

**Email Tools:**
- Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or similar
- Automation workflows
- Segmentation (B2B vs B2C)

#### 3. Content Marketing
**Blog Content Strategy:**
- Weekly blog posts (minimum 2/month)
- Topics:
  - Event planning tips
  - Recipe features
  - Behind-the-scenes content
  - Industry trends
  - Client spotlights
  - Dietary restriction guides

**Video Content:**
- Cooking demonstrations
- Event highlights/reels
- Client testimonials
- Event planning tips

#### 4. Consultation Process
**Initial Consultation Offer:**
- Free 15-30 minute discovery call
- Needs assessment questionnaire
- Custom service recommendations

**Consultation Booking:**
- Online calendar booking (Calendly, Acuity)
- Clear availability display
- Automated confirmation emails
- Reminder notifications

### Conversion Goals (Stage 2)
- Email subscribers: 200-500 (Year 1)
- Consultation bookings: 15-25/month
- Content engagement: 50%+ open rates on emails

### Key Metrics
- Email open rates (target: 25%+)
- Click-through rates (target: 3%+)
- Consultation booking rate
- Lead magnet download rates
- Time to consultation (days from inquiry)

## Stage 3: CONSIDERATION (Bottom of Funnel - BOFU)

### Objective
Convert interested prospects into booked clients

### Sales Process Elements

#### 1. Consultation & Needs Assessment
**Consultation Framework:**
- Event type and vision discussion
- Guest count and budget range
- Dietary restrictions and preferences
- Timeline and availability
- Service package recommendations

**Consultation Tools:**
- Client intake form (pre-consultation)
- Event planning questionnaire
- Budget calculator tool (if applicable)
- Visual inspiration boards/examples

#### 2. Proposal Development
**Proposal Components:**
- Detailed service description
- Custom menu (for chef services)
- Event timeline (for planning services)
- Vendor recommendations (if applicable)
- Transparent pricing breakdown
- Terms and conditions
- Next steps and timeline

**Proposal Delivery:**
- Professional PDF format
- Follow-up within 24-48 hours
- Personal presentation (if needed)
- Revisions based on feedback

#### 3. Social Proof & Trust Building
**Testimonials & Reviews:**
- Client testimonials on website
- Google Reviews request
- Yelp reviews
- Social media reviews
- Video testimonials

**Portfolio & Case Studies:**
- Photo galleries of past events
- Detailed case studies
- Before/after event transformations
- Client success stories

**Credentials & Certifications:**
- Professional certifications displayed
- Industry affiliations
- Awards and recognition
- Insurance information

#### 4. Pricing Strategy
**Clear Pricing Display:**
- Service package ranges (not exact to allow customization)
- Transparent pricing philosophy
- Value proposition explanation
- Payment terms and options

**Package Options:**
- Intimate Dining Experience: $800-$1,500
- Celebration Package: $3,000-$8,000
- Corporate Experience: $2,000-$10,000
- Custom quotes for unique needs

**Incentives:**
- Early booking discounts
- Package discounts (combined services)
- Referral discounts (10-15%)
- Off-season promotions

### Conversion Goals (Stage 3)
- Proposal acceptance rate: 40-60%
- Average time from consultation to booking: 7-14 days
- Booking conversion rate: 25-40% of consultations

### Key Metrics
- Consultation-to-proposal rate
- Proposal acceptance rate
- Average proposal value
- Sales cycle length (days)
- Objection handling success rate

---

## Stage 4: ACTION (Purchase/Booking)

### Objective
Complete the booking and payment process

### Booking Process

#### 1. Contract & Agreement
**Contract Components:**
- Service scope and deliverables
- Event details (date, time, location, guest count)
- Menu and service specifications
- Pricing and payment terms
- Cancellation policy
- Liability and insurance information
- Terms and conditions

**Contract Execution:**
- Digital signature capability (DocuSign, HelloSign)
- Email delivery
- Confirmation and receipt

#### 2. Payment Processing
**Payment Structure:**
- Deposit: 50% at booking
- Final payment: 50% before/at event (as specified in contract)

**Payment Methods:**
- Credit/debit cards (Stripe, Square, PayPal)
- Bank transfer
- Check (if acceptable)

**Payment Tools:**
- Online invoice system
- Automated payment reminders
- Receipt generation

#### 3. Pre-Event Communication
**Event Preparation:**
- Pre-event planning meetings (1-2 weeks before)
- Menu finalization
- Timeline confirmation
- Vendor coordination (if applicable)
- Final details confirmation

**Communication Touchpoints:**
- Confirmation email upon booking
- Event reminder (1 week before)
- Final confirmation (48 hours before)
- Day-of check-in

### Conversion Goals (Stage 4)
- Booking completion rate: 95%+ (of proposals accepted)
- Payment collection rate: 100% (with proper contracts)
- On-time payment rate: 90%+

### Key Metrics
- Booking completion rate
- Average payment processing time
- Payment collection rate
- Contract execution time
- Pre-event meeting completion rate

---

## Stage 5: RETENTION (Post-Purchase)

### Objective
Ensure client satisfaction and generate repeat business

### Post-Event Process

#### 1. Service Delivery Excellence
**During Event:**
- Professional service execution
- Attention to detail
- Problem-solving responsiveness
- Client check-ins

**Post-Event:**
- Follow-up communication (within 24-48 hours)
- Thank you note/card
- Client feedback request

#### 2. Review & Testimonial Collection
**Review Requests:**
- Automated follow-up email (2-3 days post-event)
- Review links (Google, Yelp, Facebook)
- Testimonial form on website
- Video testimonial request (for select clients)

**Incentives:**
- Small discount on next booking for reviews
- Social media feature (with permission)

#### 3. Client Retention Campaigns
**Repeat Client Outreach:**
- Anniversary of first event
- Holiday promotions
- Special offers for past clients
- Newsletter with new services/updates

**Referral Program:**
- Referral discount (10-15% off)
- Referral rewards for referrer
- Easy referral process
- Tracking system

#### 4. Upsell & Cross-Sell Opportunities
**Additional Services:**
- Cooking classes for groups
- Meal prep services
- Additional event planning services
- Gift certificates

**Timing:**
- During consultation (package upsells)
- Post-event follow-up (future events)
- Seasonal promotions

### Conversion Goals (Stage 5)
- Client satisfaction rate: 90%+
- Review collection rate: 60%+
- Repeat client rate: 30-40% (Year 2+)
- Referral generation: 2-3 referrals per 10 clients

### Key Metrics
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Client satisfaction scores
- Review/rating averages
- Repeat booking rate
- Referral rate
- Customer lifetime value (CLV)

## Sales Funnel KPIs & Metrics Dashboard

### Overall Funnel Metrics

#### Awareness Stage
- Monthly website visitors: 500-1,000 (Year 1)
- Social media reach: 5,000+ (combined)
- Cost per visitor: $1-$5
- Brand awareness score

#### Interest Stage
- Monthly email subscribers: 50-100 new

- Lead magnet conversion: 5-10%

#### Consideration Stage
- Consultation-to-proposal rate: 60-80%

- Average proposal value: $3,000-$5,000
- Sales cycle length: 14-30 days

#### Action Stage
- Booking completion rate: 95%+
- Payment collection rate: 100%

- Monthly bookings: 4-8 events (Year 1, scaling up)

#### Retention Stage
- Client satisfaction: 90%+
- Review rate: 60%+
- Repeat client rate: 30-40% (Year 2+)
- Referral rate: 20-30% of clients

### Financial Metrics
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): $200-$500
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): $5,000-$15,000
- CLV:CAC ratio: 10:1 or better
- Monthly recurring revenue (if applicable)
- Average revenue per client
